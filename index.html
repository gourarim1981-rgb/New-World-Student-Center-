How It Works

From enrollment to
exam success in 4 steps

A clear, structured journey — no guesswork, no wasted time. Just a proven path that gets you to Germany.

1
Free Level Assessment
We evaluate your current German level and learning goals in a short, relaxed conversation — then recommend the right course and schedule for you.
2
Structured Classes with Certified Teachers
Small groups, clear weekly milestones, and real classroom interaction — in-person at our Fès center or through live online sessions that match your schedule.
3
Official Exam Preparation
Targeted mock exams, exam technique coaching, and practice under real test conditions — for Goethe, telc, and OSD certification at every level from A1 to B2.
4
Career & Immigration Guidance
Once certified, we help you navigate the next steps: Ausbildung applications, university admission requirements, visa language documentation, and job search strategies in Germany.
